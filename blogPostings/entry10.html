Let's begin with some basic definitions.

  • Definitions:

    • Risk-free Assets: Assets with a fixed outcome essentially, we know there is not much volatility with said asset in advance. Ex: bank deposits, bonds.

    Risky Assets: the opposite of a risk-free Asset, there outcome is unknown and could go up our down based on probability. Ex: stocks, options, commodities.

    Random Variable: A mathematical Quantity or object that depends on random events. Ex: flipping a coin, it could land heads or tails, the probability of each is 50/50

    Position: The number of shares of the assest held by an investor.
